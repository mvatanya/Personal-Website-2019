About Me

I'm an inspired web developer with a mechanical engineer background. I attained a Bachelors of Automotive Design and Manufacturing Engineering from Chulalongkorn University (Thailand) in 2011. Then I worked as a mechanical design engineer in Japan for several years before I decided to come to the United States to pursue my master's degree. I then graduated my Masters of Engineering Management from the University of Southern California in 2017. Moving to the United States has opened up my world on the web technology side and I started to become interested in learning how to code. I started teaching myself Python and use it to manipulate complex data, perform exploratory analysis and build predictive models using implementations of machine learning algorithms.

After months of teaching myself Python and data manipulation I found a job as a Bilingual Engineer at Google. I worked with Google's internal tools to provide better search coverage for different locales in different verticals. I gathered thousands of queries and I analyzed them in order to be able to perform loss analysis and find solutions to improve the search coverage. I became even more passionate about web technology from this work and therefore I decided to put a hundred percent of my focus on learning how to become a professional software engineer by joining a coding bootcamp, Rithm.

I have always had a passion for problem solving and engineering. I enjoy building things and fixing things, so if you have the same interst as I do, contact me!
