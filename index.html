About Me

Hey there! I'm thrilled to share my story as I transition back to the workforce as a Frontend Engineer, following a year-long break to care for my newborn. My professional path has been diverse with valuable experiences.

My most recent role as a Senior Frontend Engineer at Stacklet was an exciting venture. I was the first frontend engineer and played a pivotal role in building the Stacklet UI using React, Relay, GraphQL, and MaterialUI, handling authentication and developing an internal component library. My collaboration with designers and product managers was instrumental in crafting a user-centric experience.

Before this, at Fountain, I contributed as a Software Engineer, enhancing user experiences through SPA development, performance improvements, and mobile responsiveness. I've also worked as a Frontend Engineer on contract, building admin applications, conducting rigorous testing, and implementing user authentication.

My journey started at Google as a Bilingual Engineer, where I worked to improve search results for different locales and verticals. I also contributed to search algorithm enhancements.

Beyond my software roles, I have a solid foundation in Mechanical Design Engineering, honed during my tenure at Konan Sekkei. My academic background includes a Master of Science in Engineering Management from the University of Southern California. I earned my Bachelor's in Automotive Design and Manufacturing Engineering from Chulalongkorn University in Thailand.

I'm passionate about innovation, problem-solving, and building impactful user experiences. My unique blend of experiences and skills is ready to make a positive contribution to your team and projects!
